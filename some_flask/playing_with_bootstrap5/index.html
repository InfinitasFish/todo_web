Hello, bootstrap!

So each container in bootstrap contains 12 units of length

Example 1:
col1 6 units
col2 4 units
col3 2 units

As you can see. But after a breakpoint, in case of 'md' it's 768px, container and its columns will take 100% of length

Example 2:
col sm 8 units
col sm 4 units

In this case col have own breakpoints, so after shrinking they will collapse into 1 column. In other words, breakpoint defines a min-width.

Example 3:
col lg 8 units -> col sm 6 units
col lg 4 units -> col sm 6 units

Also we can combine column breakpoint to make more complex grids for different use cases
